### Concepts Locked In
- Ethernet is a collection of network standards and protocols
- Data speed is measure in bits, while data itself is measured in bytes
- Full Duplex Transmission: pins route Rx data & Tx data simultaneously
- Crossover Cables must be used on (typically outdated devices) identical devices such as a switch connecting to a switch (Because their Tx & Rx Pins are the same causing crosstalk)
- Straight Through cables are used on devices who have opposite Tx & Rx Pins
- Auto MDI-X is a feauture designed to auto detect which pins transmit vs receive; and adjust accordingly. Modern switches use this to make crossover cables obselete
- Differences between Multi-Mode Fiber and Single-Mode Fiber
